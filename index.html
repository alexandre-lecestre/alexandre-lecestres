Alexandre Lecestre, PhD candidate in Statistics

About me

I started my PhD in September 2019 at the University of Luxembourg, as a member of the SanDAL team, under the supervision of Prof. Yannick Baraud. My work focuses on robust estimation, non i.i.d. settings, mixture models, hidden Markov models, supremum of empirical processes. I will defend my PhD in December 2023.

Papers and preprints

  • Robust estimation in finite mixture models. (2023, ESAIM:PS )
